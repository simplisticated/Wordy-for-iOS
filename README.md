## At a Glance

`Wordy` is a powerful text processor that provides an easy way to manage content in `String` object.

## How To Get Started

- Copy content of `Source` folder to your project.

or

- Use `Wordy` cocoapod

## Requirements

* iOS 9 and later
* Xcode 9 and later
* Swift 4
